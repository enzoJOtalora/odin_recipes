Milanesa

Description

Fried or baked, milanesa is an easy success for any lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • A thin cut of meat (beef, pork, chicken
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Provencal mix
  • Sesame oil or similar

Steps

  1. Pass the meat through an egg and provencal mix
  2. Pass trough bread while shaking off excess bread
  3. Bake or fry in vegetable oil
